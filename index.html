Liz Flynn

One half of Threadwinners


Liz Flynn is a fiber artist based in Orange County, California. In partnership with Alyssa Arney, their crochet collaborative Threadwinners utilizes the vehicle of crochet to create large-scale sculptural pieces. They have exhibited in galleries, universities, and collaborative art installations across the US.
